  • The Legend of Zelda (Unity Remaster) (with Vincent Le)
    Try it out!

    • Quick Start:
      • WASD for movement
      • X for primary weapon, Z for secondary
      • Press 1 to toggle invincibility, 4 to toggle stages
    • Was responsible for some administrative set-up, as well as core ai design and weapon/enemy design while recreating the core game.
      For our additional mechanics, I was responsible for implementing a majority of mechanical interactions with enemies, and some UI.
    • The technologies used were C# for scripting and Unity as our game engine. Github was used a version management and Jira for organization.
    • Shoutout to my group member for excellent level design and insane bug-finding skills!

  • Sunrise in 66 Seconds
    Try it out!

    • Quick Start:
      • WASD for movement
      • Left Mouse for primary weapon, E and R for secondarys
      • Press Space to toggle slow-down mode
    • The P2 solo project, resembles a clone of games such as Vampire Survivors or 20 Minutes Till Dawn, but with a time-slow mechanic linked to a power resource. We note the resolution may not be optimal, but should be playable on most devices.
